Engineering is the application of science and math to design, test, and build machines, structures, and processes. Engineers use their skills to solve real-world problems and improve the world around us.

Mechanical engineering: A popular branch of engineering. Mechanical engineering courses are related to physics, heat transfer, thermodynamics, fluid dynamics, and IC engines.

Computer science engineering: Involves designing and prototyping computing devices and systems.

Civil engineering: Involves the planning, construction, maintenance, and supervision of infrastructure. Civil engineering has subdisciplines like municipal engineering, geotechnical engineering, structural engineering, and transport engineering
